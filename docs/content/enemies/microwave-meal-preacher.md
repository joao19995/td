# Microwave Meal Preacher

*"Why wait twelve hours when you can wait two minutes?"*

> — Flavor: "Two-minute meals. Lifetime regret."

Once respected bakers, these zealots abandoned fermentation in favor of convenience.

They travel from town to town preaching the Gospel of Instant Meals, claiming flavor is merely a social construct.

The Order considers them among the saddest victims of industrial influence.

Behind every sermon is someone who forgot what real bread tastes like.
