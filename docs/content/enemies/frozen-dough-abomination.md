# Frozen Dough Abomination

*"Proofing? Never heard of it."*

> — Flavor: "Proofing was optional. Apparently."

Created in the deepest freezers beneath forgotten supermarket warehouses, this creature is made from countless batches of dough that never had the chance to rise.

Cold, dense and unnaturally heavy, it lumbers forward with only one purpose:

To convince the world that frozen dough is "just as good."

No Sage has ever managed to explain how it is still alive.
