# Dough Exorcist — "Purifier of Corrupted Gluten"

*"Every soul can be saved... some simply require stronger persuasion."*

The Dough Exorcists are the last resort of the Order of the Mother Dough. They are dispatched only when a soul has been consumed entirely by industrial baking. Carrying blessed flour, sacred starters and ancient rites of purification, they do not seek vengeance—they seek redemption. But when redemption comes too late, judgment follows.

Some souls fall too deeply into processed bread.

The Order believes these victims are possessed by the Spirit of Preservatives.

Only Dough Exorcists possess the rituals required to remove this corruption.

They throw blessed flour into the air while reciting the Forty-Two Proofing Verses.

Most victims recover peacefully.

The truly corrupted resist until their strength is nearly exhausted.

Only then can the Exorcist deliver the Final Knead, breaking the industrial influence forever.

The Order insists this is an act of mercy.

The victims usually agree afterward.

### Level I — Rite Initiate

*"Every purification begins with faith."*

Before performing their first exorcism, every Initiate spends years studying the ancient rites of fermentation and learning to recognize the signs of industrial corruption.

Most never forget their first successful conversion.

### Level II — Blessed Flour

Cost: 143g — +10 Damage, +0.2 Fire Rate

*"Even flour can become holy."*

The Exorcist is entrusted with blessed flour milled from wheat grown within the sacred gardens of the Order. Each handful burns away traces of artificial preservatives and weakens even the strongest heretics.

### Level III — Judgment Aura

Cost: 209g — +15 Damage, +0.3 Fire Rate

*"Mercy ends where corruption begins."*

After mastering the sacred rites, the Exorcist radiates an invisible presence that unsettles corrupted souls. Every strike carries the weight of countless generations who defended the Mother Dough before them.

The guilty often lower their gaze before the first attack.

### Level IV — High Exorcist

Cost: 286g — +20 Damage, +0.3 Fire Rate

*"The strongest corruption demands the strongest conviction."*

High Exorcists are entrusted with cleansing entire cities overtaken by industrial bread. Their ceremonies combine ancient prayers, living sourdough cultures and sacred flour into rituals feared by every servant of processed baking.

Many enemies surrender before judgment is pronounced.

### Level V — Grand Purifier of the Mother Dough

Cost: 396g — +30 Damage, +0.4 Fire Rate

*"No corruption survives the First Starter."*

Only a handful of Grand Purifiers have ever walked beside the Mother Dough.

Their sacred starter is said to descend directly from the First Oven, carrying a purity so overwhelming that even the oldest heresies crumble in its presence.

When the Grand Purifier raises a hand... judgment has already been delivered.

## Synergies

### Crust Judgment Protocol

*"The Crusader breaks the crust. The Exorcist removes what remains."*

This forbidden battlefield doctrine was used only against the worst industrial-bread warlords. A critical strike exposes the corruption, and the Exorcist delivers immediate judgment.

**Effect**: Crit vs target <50% HP = instant execute (9999 dmg), 10s cooldown per tower. Most powerful boss-killer synergy.

### Grand Opening Rush

*"When every craft joins the feast, the streets move faster."*

The Order celebrates new bakeries with a chaotic festival involving monks, couriers, bakers, sages, and trucks all working at once. Nobody knows who is in charge, but bread appears everywhere.

**Effect**: +10% fire rate to all towers if 3+ tower types are on the map.
