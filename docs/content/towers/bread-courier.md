# Bread Courier — "The Messengers of Freshness"

*"Bread delayed is faith denied."*

The Bread Courier is the fastest member of the Order. While others perfect recipes, Couriers dedicate their lives to speed, believing that a loaf delivered warm carries far greater power than one delivered late. Every promotion makes them faster, more precise, and seemingly impossible to outrun.

The Couriers are responsible for delivering warm bread across the kingdom before the crust loses its sacred crackle.

Legend says the first Courier outran a horse while carrying twelve loaves balanced on his head, refusing to let even one cool before reaching a starving village.

Members of the Order joke that Couriers have forgotten how to walk.

Their bicycles are blessed every spring using olive oil and sourdough steam. Each messenger memorizes every road, shortcut, alleyway and suspicious supermarket loading dock.

Some claim Couriers can smell industrial bread from several kilometers away.

Whenever a village receives fresh bread before sunrise, the Courier leaves without accepting thanks.

Only crumbs remain.

### Level I — Fresh Recruit

*"No village should wait for fresh bread."*

Every Courier begins with a simple bicycle, a sturdy delivery bag, and a promise to never let a loaf arrive cold. Their journey starts with endless roads and countless deliveries.

### Level II — Overclock

Cost: 88g — +0.5 Fire Rate

*"Pedal faster. The bread won't wait."*

The Courier upgrades their trusty bicycle with reinforced gears and an enchanted chain blessed by the Order's mechanics. Every delivery becomes swifter, allowing more loaves to reach their destination before the crust cools.

### Level III — Precision Rounds

Cost: 132g — +10 Damage, +0.4 Fire Rate

*"Accuracy is faster than hesitation."*

Years of practice teach the Courier exactly where every loaf should land. Missed deliveries become impossible, and each throw strikes with surprising force.

Senior Couriers proudly claim they can hit a supermarket shopper from across a marketplace without spilling a single crumb.

### Level IV — Master Courier

Cost: 198g — +15 Damage, +0.5 Fire Rate

*"Every shortcut is already memorized."*

Master Couriers know every road, alleyway, hidden staircase and bakery in the kingdom. Their routes are so perfectly optimized that they often arrive before people realize they ordered bread.

Even the Grocery Run Joggers struggle to keep up.

### Level V — Herald of the Mother Dough

Cost: 286g — +20 Damage, +0.6 Fire Rate

*"The wind itself carries the bread."*

Only the greatest Couriers earn the title of Herald. Legends say they no longer ride through the kingdom—they become part of its roads.

Wherever a Herald appears, warm bread follows moments later.

Some believe they have delivered so many loaves that time itself now makes room for them.

## Synergies

### Grand Opening Rush

*"When every craft joins the feast, the streets move faster."*

The Order celebrates new bakeries with a chaotic festival involving monks, couriers, bakers, sages, and trucks all working at once. Nobody knows who is in charge, but bread appears everywhere.

**Effect**: The excitement accelerates everyone's work — +10% fire rate to all towers if 3+ tower types are on the map.
