# The Gluten Null Bishop

*"The Great Denier."*

> — Flavor: "Faith expires. Preservatives don't."

No enemy is feared more by the Order.

The Bishop teaches that tradition has no value, fermentation is unnecessary and that recipes should always be "optimized."

His sermons drain faith from nearby bakers, causing their blessings to weaken.

Some scholars believe he never truly believed in bread at all. He merely believes in efficiency.

To the Order, that is infinitely worse.
