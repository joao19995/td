# Supermarket Overlord of White Bread

*"Chairman of Convenience."*

> — Flavor: "CEO of Convenience."

The Overlord controls entire supermarket chains through aggressive discounts, loyalty cards and suspiciously inexpensive sandwich loaves.

Every industrial bakery reports directly to them.

Their greatest weapon is not strength — it's convincing people that "all bread tastes the same."

The Order considers this statement the highest form of heresy.
