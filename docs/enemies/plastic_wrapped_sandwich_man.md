# Plastic Wrapped Sandwich Man

*"Freshness guaranteed until the end of time."*

> — Flavor: "Guaranteed fresh until the heat death of the universe."

No one remembers his real name.

After years spent promoting vacuum-sealed sandwiches, he eventually wrapped himself in protective plastic "for maximum freshness."

The wrapping became permanent.

Nothing enters.

Nothing escapes.

Not even good flavor.

Some claim he squeaks when he walks.
