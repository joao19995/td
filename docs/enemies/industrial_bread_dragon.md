# The Industrial Bread Dragon

*"The First Oven That Forgot How To Ferment."*

> — Flavor: "Its fire smells suspiciously artificial."

Legends say the Dragon was once an ancient bakery guardian.

When greed replaced patience, it consumed preservatives, artificial flavors and endless streams of commercial yeast until it transformed into the living embodiment of industrial baking.

Its wings scatter flour that never ferments.

Its breath smells faintly of preservatives.

Entire kingdoms abandoned artisan bread after seeing it.

The High Prophet teaches that defeating the Dragon does not destroy it — it simply remembers how to bake again.
