# Sliced Bread Tourist

*"Just passing through... and stopping at every supermarket."*

> — Flavor: "Looking for authentic cuisine. Eating packaged bread."

Most tourists arrive with good intentions.

Unfortunately, they often fall victim to the convenience of pre-sliced bread sold at every souvenir shop and highway convenience store.

The Order believes these poor travelers simply have never tasted proper sourdough.

Many are converted before finishing their vacation.

*"I only wanted something for the road..."*
