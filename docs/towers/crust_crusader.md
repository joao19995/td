# Crust Crusader — "Knight of the Golden Crust"

*"Where words fail, the crust speaks."*

The Crust Crusaders are the holy knights of the Order of the Mother Dough. Armed with blades tempered in olive oil and shields forged from ancient baking stones, they march fearlessly against the greatest heresies. Every victory hardens their resolve, and every perfectly baked crust strengthens both weapon and spirit.

The Crusaders are elite warriors chosen after successfully baking one thousand consecutive loaves without burning a single crust.

Their swords are forged from folded steel repeatedly coated with olive oil before being baked inside sacred ovens.

They believe the crust represents discipline.

Without crust, bread collapses.

Without discipline, civilization follows.

Every successful strike is accompanied by the unmistakable sound of perfectly breaking crust.

Many enemies surrender simply from hearing it.

### Level I — Crust Squire

*"Every crusade begins with a single loaf."*

Young Crusaders begin as squires, entrusted with guarding village bakeries and escorting sacred starters between monasteries. Their weapons are humble, but their conviction is unwavering.

### Level II — Sharpened Crust

Cost: 120g — +10 Damage, +0.2 Fire Rate

*"A sharper edge cuts through thicker heresy."*

The Crusader reforges their blade using the legendary techniques of the Order's smiths, tempering it in extra virgin olive oil and polishing it with coarse sea salt.

Every strike lands cleaner, faster and with greater purpose.

### Level III — Holy Crusher

Cost: 170g — +15 Damage, +0.3 Fire Rate

*"Some loaves cannot be sliced. They must be shattered."*

Veteran Crusaders exchange elegant swords for enormous crust hammers capable of breaking the toughest industrial creations. Every crushing blow sends crumbs flying across the battlefield.

Even Heavy enemies begin to falter.

### Level IV — Knight of the Golden Crust

Cost: 240g — +20 Damage, +0.3 Fire Rate

*"A knight's oath is baked into every crust."*

After years of faithful service, the Crusader is knighted beneath the Great Oven of the Order. Their armor is adorned with golden wheat, and every victory becomes another chapter in the chronicles of the Mother Dough.

To face a Golden Knight is to face centuries of tradition.

### Level V — Grand Crusader of the First Oven

Cost: 340g — +25 Damage, +0.4 Fire Rate

*"Their blade remembers the First Bake."*

Only the greatest champions ever receive this title.

Legend claims their weapon was forged using fragments of the First Baking Stone and cooled with water from the original fermentation well. Every critical strike echoes with the strength of generations of bakers who came before.

Even the Gluten Null Bishop fears the sound of their approaching footsteps.

## Synergies

### Crust Judgment Protocol

*"The Crusader breaks the crust. The Exorcist removes what remains."*

This forbidden battlefield doctrine was used only against the worst industrial-bread warlords. A critical strike exposes the corruption, and the Exorcist delivers immediate judgment.

**Effect**: Crit vs target <50% HP = instant execute (9999 dmg), 10s cooldown per tower. Most powerful boss-killer synergy. Ignores Gluten Null Bishop's anti-buff aura (depends on Crit, not % damage).

### Grand Opening Rush

*"When every craft joins the feast, the streets move faster."*

The Order celebrates new bakeries with a chaotic festival involving monks, couriers, bakers, sages, and trucks all working at once. Nobody knows who is in charge, but bread appears everywhere.

**Effect**: +10% fire rate to all towers if 3+ tower types are on the map.
