# Aroma Keeper — "Guardian of the Sacred Fragrance"

*"Even unbelievers cannot outrun the smell of truth."*

The Aroma Keepers are guardians of the Sacred Fragrance, carrying ancient clay vessels filled with living aromas cultivated over decades. Their purpose is not to defeat enemies through force, but to slow their march long enough for the Mother Dough to reach their hearts. With every promotion, their mastery over scent and fermentation grows stronger.

Rather than fighting directly, they carry enchanted clay jars containing aromas cultivated for decades inside underground fermentation vaults.

When opened, the fragrance awakens forgotten memories.

Children remember baking with grandparents.

Travelers remember home.

Even hardened followers of sliced bread instinctively slow their steps, overwhelmed by nostalgia.

Scholars debate whether the aroma is magical or simply the smell of exceptionally good bread.

The Aroma Keepers refuse to answer.

### Level I — Fragrance Novice

*"Every scent tells a story."*

Newly initiated Aroma Keepers spend years learning to identify herbs, spices and fermentation notes before earning the right to carry a Sacred Aroma Vessel.

Their first lesson is simple: *"People may ignore words, but never a fresh loaf."*

### Level II — Cryo Coil

Cost: 80g — +0.3 Fire Rate

*"The colder the air, the longer the aroma lingers."*

By wrapping their vessels with enchanted cooling coils, the Keeper preserves the fragrance for longer periods. The aroma spreads more consistently, allowing them to release sacred scents more frequently.

### Level III — Permafrost

Cost: 120g — +10 Damage, +0.2 Fire Rate

*"A scent frozen in time."*

After years of experimentation, the Keeper learns to preserve fermentation aromas indefinitely within magical frost. Every release carries a sharper, more concentrated wave of divine fragrance that weakens even the most determined followers of industrial bread.

### Level IV — Master of Aromas

Cost: 180g — +15 Damage, +0.2 Fire Rate

*"One breath can awaken forgotten memories."*

Master Keepers can recreate the scent of any bread ever baked by the Order. Their fragrances become so vivid that enemies pause instinctively, reminded of childhood kitchens, warm ovens and forgotten family recipes.

Entire crowds have reportedly stopped marching simply to inhale.

### Level V — Keeper of the Eternal Scent

Cost: 260g — +20 Damage, +0.3 Fire Rate

*"The fragrance of the First Oven still lingers."*

Only a select few Keepers ever master the Eternal Scent—a mythical aroma believed to originate from the very first loaf baked by the Mother Dough.

Those who encounter it are overwhelmed by memories that were never their own.

Even the oldest heretics hesitate before taking another step.

## Synergies

### One Whiff, One Bite

*"No soul survives the scent and the sample."*

During the Siege of Whitebread Square, an Aroma Keeper filled the streets with the fragrance of fresh sourdough while a Taste Tester offered free samples from a silver tray. Entire enemy battalions abandoned their packaged bread and joined the Order before the battle even began.

**Effect**: +15% damage to both towers. Slow keeps targets in range longer for more poison ticks.

### Holy Fermentation Network

*"Fermentation spreads farther than fear."*

Scholars discovered that sacred aromas could travel through the same invisible currents used by living yeast cultures. When an Aroma Keeper and a Fermentation Sage work together, conversion spreads through crowds like a chain reaction.

**Effect**: +30% range for slow/chain spread.

### Grand Opening Rush

*"When every craft joins the feast, the streets move faster."*

The Order celebrates new bakeries with a chaotic festival involving monks, couriers, bakers, sages, and trucks all working at once. Nobody knows who is in charge, but bread appears everywhere.

**Effect**: +10% fire rate to all towers if 3+ tower types are on the map.
