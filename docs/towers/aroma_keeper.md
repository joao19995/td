# Aroma Keeper — "Guardian of the Sacred Fragrance"

*"Even unbelievers cannot outrun the smell of truth."*

The Aroma Keepers preserve the legendary scent of perfect fermentation.

Rather than fighting directly, they carry enchanted clay jars containing aromas cultivated for decades inside underground fermentation vaults.

When opened, the fragrance awakens forgotten memories.

Children remember baking with grandparents.

Travelers remember home.

Even hardened followers of sliced bread instinctively slow their steps, overwhelmed by nostalgia.

Scholars debate whether the aroma is magical or simply the smell of exceptionally good bread.

The Aroma Keepers refuse to answer.

## Synergies

### One Whiff, One Bite

*"No soul survives the scent and the sample."*

During the Siege of Whitebread Square, an Aroma Keeper filled the streets with the fragrance of fresh sourdough while a Taste Tester offered free samples from a silver tray. Entire enemy battalions abandoned their packaged bread and joined the Order before the battle even began.

**Effect**: +15% damage to both towers. Slow keeps targets in range longer for more poison ticks.

### Holy Fermentation Network

*"Fermentation spreads farther than fear."*

Scholars discovered that sacred aromas could travel through the same invisible currents used by living yeast cultures. When an Aroma Keeper and a Fermentation Sage work together, conversion spreads through crowds like a chain reaction.

**Effect**: +30% range for slow/chain spread.

### Grand Opening Rush

*"When every craft joins the feast, the streets move faster."*

The Order celebrates new bakeries with a chaotic festival involving monks, couriers, bakers, sages, and trucks all working at once. Nobody knows who is in charge, but bread appears everywhere.

**Effect**: +10% fire rate to all towers if 3+ tower types are on the map.
