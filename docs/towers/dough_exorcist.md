# Dough Exorcist — "Purifier of Corrupted Gluten"

*"The industrial spirit must be removed."*

Some souls fall too deeply into processed bread.

The Order believes these victims are possessed by the Spirit of Preservatives.

Only Dough Exorcists possess the rituals required to remove this corruption.

They throw blessed flour into the air while reciting the Forty-Two Proofing Verses.

Most victims recover peacefully.

The truly corrupted resist until their strength is nearly exhausted.

Only then can the Exorcist deliver the Final Knead, breaking the industrial influence forever.

The Order insists this is an act of mercy.

The victims usually agree afterward.

## Synergies

### Crust Judgment Protocol

*"The Crusader breaks the crust. The Exorcist removes what remains."*

This forbidden battlefield doctrine was used only against the worst industrial-bread warlords. A critical strike exposes the corruption, and the Exorcist delivers immediate judgment.

**Effect**: Crit vs target <50% HP = instant execute (9999 dmg), 10s cooldown per tower. Most powerful boss-killer synergy.

### Grand Opening Rush

*"When every craft joins the feast, the streets move faster."*

The Order celebrates new bakeries with a chaotic festival involving monks, couriers, bakers, sages, and trucks all working at once. Nobody knows who is in charge, but bread appears everywhere.

**Effect**: +10% fire rate to all towers if 3+ tower types are on the map.
