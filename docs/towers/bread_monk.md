# Bread Monk — "The Silent Fermenters"

*"The dough rises in silence."*

The Bread Monks devote their lives to meditation, discipline and the sacred rhythm of fermentation. They rarely seek glory on the battlefield, instead strengthening the resolve of every brother fighting beside them. As they ascend through the Order, their chants grow more inspiring, their presence more calming, and their faith becomes a beacon that unites the entire bakery.

Bread Monks spend years inside mountain monasteries where speaking is forbidden.

Communication happens entirely through kneading techniques.

Masters can identify another monk's emotional state simply by watching them fold dough.

Rather than fighting themselves, they strengthen the spirit of nearby brothers.

Their chants synchronize heartbeats, breathing and fermentation cycles until everyone works in perfect harmony.

Some monks have meditated so long that birds mistake them for statues.

No monk has ever explained the Prayer Beads of Gluten.

Everyone is too afraid to ask.

### Level I — Silent Disciple

*"Words are fleeting. Dough remembers."*

Every Bread Monk begins with years of silence inside the mountain monasteries of the Order. They learn to communicate through gestures, breathing and the rhythm of kneading alone.

Only after mastering patience are they allowed onto the battlefield.

### Level II — Sacred Chant

Cost: 100g — +8 Damage, +0.2 Fire Rate

*"Every verse strengthens the faithful."*

The Disciple learns the First Hymns of Fermentation, ancient chants sung in perfect rhythm with the kneading of dough. Their calm voice inspires nearby brothers while allowing the Monk to strike with renewed confidence.

### Level III — Devotion Aura

Cost: 140g — +12 Damage, +0.3 Fire Rate

*"Faith shared is faith multiplied."*

Years of meditation deepen the Monk's spiritual presence. Their chants become impossible to ignore, filling the battlefield with unwavering devotion.

Though the sacred aura itself does not grow stronger, the Monk's own conviction allows them to fight with greater purpose.

### Level IV — Abbot of Fermentation

Cost: 200g — +15 Damage, +0.3 Fire Rate

*"A calm heart steadies every hand."*

Only the wisest monks earn the title of Abbot. They oversee entire monasteries, preserving ancient recipes and guiding younger brothers through the sacred traditions of slow fermentation.

Simply standing near an Abbot reminds the faithful why they fight.

### Level V — Grandmaster of the Sacred Crust

Cost: 280g — +20 Damage, +0.4 Fire Rate

*"Even silence can become a sermon."*

The Grandmasters have transcended spoken prayer altogether.

Their mere presence fills the air with peace, discipline and the comforting scent of freshly baked bread. Legends claim entire armies have lowered their weapons simply by meeting the gaze of one such Monk.

No sermon was ever needed.

*"When every craft joins the feast, the streets move faster."*
