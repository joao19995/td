# Bakery Truck — "The Rolling Cathedral"

*"Why defend one village when you can bring the bakery to the battlefield?"*

Originally built to supply remote villages with fresh bread, the Bakery Trucks quickly became the Order's most devastating siege engines. Every promotion adds larger ovens, stronger flour cannons and increasingly unstable baking techniques. By the end of their journey, they are less a vehicle... and more a rolling festival of explosive fermentation.

During the Great Supermarket Wars, the Order realized fixed bakeries were easy targets.

Their solution was simple: put the bakery on wheels.

Each Bakery Truck contains a full wood-fired oven, flour storage, proofing shelves and emergency butter reserves.

The heat inside is so intense that nearby enemies are struck by waves of fresh bread, flying crusts and exploding flour sacks.

Children cheer when one arrives.

Industrial bakers panic.

Insurance companies refuse to cover them.

### Level I — Rolling Bakery

*"Fresh bread, wherever it is needed."*

Every Bakery Truck begins as a modest mobile oven, delivering warm loaves to villages beyond the reach of traditional bakeries.

Its crew quickly learns that fresh bread can be just as effective against heretics as it is against hunger.

### Level II — Explosive Charge

Cost: 132g — +15 Damage

*"Too much pressure. Just enough baking."*

Engineers of the Order reinforce the firing oven with pressure chambers that launch loaves at incredible speed. Every impact bursts into a cloud of crumbs, flour and overwhelming aroma.

The insurance guild has repeatedly asked the Order to stop building these.

The Order has politely declined.

### Level III — Nova Blast

Cost: 176g — +20 Damage, +12 Range

*"One loaf becomes a festival."*

The truck's ovens are upgraded with ceremonial fermentation chambers capable of producing enormous explosive loaves. The blasts can now reach crowds well beyond the front lines.

Entire town squares have reportedly been converted during accidental demonstrations.

### Level IV — Festival Cannon

Cost: 242g — +25 Damage, +10 Range

*"Every battle deserves a grand opening."*

No longer just a bakery, the vehicle becomes the centerpiece of the Order's great Bread Festivals. Giant flour cannons, reinforced ovens and ceremonial banners transform every battlefield into a celebration of artisan baking.

Enemies rarely know whether they are under attack... or attending a parade.

### Level V — Cathedral on Wheels

Cost: 330g — +35 Damage, +14 Range

*"The Mother Dough travels with her faithful."*

Only a handful of these legendary wagons have ever existed.

Each carries a sacred wood-fired oven said to have been lit using embers from the First Oven itself. Every loaf launched explodes with such force that entire armies pause in awe before remembering they are supposed to be fighting.

Where the Cathedral on Wheels arrives... a bakery soon follows.

## Synergies

### Grand Opening Rush

*"When every craft joins the feast, the streets move faster."*

The Order celebrates new bakeries with a chaotic festival involving monks, couriers, bakers, sages, and trucks all working at once. Nobody knows who is in charge, but bread appears everywhere.

**Effect**: +10% fire rate to all towers if 3+ tower types are on the map.
