# Taste Tester — "The Last Judge"

*"One bite tells them everything. The poison is merely persuasive."*

Every loaf baked by the Order must first pass through the Taste Testers. Their refined palate can detect the smallest imperfection, and their "poison" is nothing more than the overwhelming realization that artisan bread exists. As they rise through the ranks, their mixtures become increasingly potent, slowly breaking even the strongest devotion to industrial baking.

Their tongues are considered sacred relics.

A Taste Tester can identify hydration percentage, fermentation time, flour origin and emotional state of the baker after a single bite.

The "poison" they use is not truly poison — it is overwhelming enlightenment.

Those who taste artisan bread suddenly realize what packaged bread has stolen from them.

This revelation spreads slowly through the body until resistance collapses.

Many enemies surrender voluntarily after their first sample.

### Level I — Junior Taster

*"A single bite reveals the baker's soul."*

Every Taste Tester begins by sampling hundreds of loaves each day. Before they learn to persuade others, they must first master every flavor the Mother Dough has to offer.

### Level II — Toxic Vial

Cost: 100g — +5 Damage

*"Every recipe needs one secret ingredient."*

The Taster begins carrying carefully prepared fermentation extracts distilled from ancient sourdough cultures. Though harmless to the faithful, they make industrial bread seem unbearably bland.

### Level III — Concentrated Venom

Cost: 140g — +8 Damage, +0.3 Fire Rate

*"One taste becomes impossible to forget."*

Years of experimentation allow the Taster to refine their persuasive mixtures into an intensely concentrated essence. The realization spreads faster through every victim, making resistance increasingly difficult.

### Level IV — Master Sommelier of Dough

Cost: 200g — +12 Damage, +0.3 Fire Rate

*"Every crumb tells a story."*

Only the finest palates in the Order earn this title. They can identify flour origin, hydration level, fermentation time and oven temperature from a single crumb.

Their recommendations are considered absolute truth among bakers.

### Level V — Grand Inquisitor of Flavor

Cost: 280g — +15 Damage, +0.4 Fire Rate

*"No tongue can deny the Mother Dough."*

The Grand Inquisitors possess a palate so refined that one bite can awaken forgotten memories of bread their victims have never even tasted.

Legends claim even the Industrial Bread Dragon once hesitated after accepting a sample.

No second sample was required.

## Synergies

### One Whiff, One Bite

*"No soul survives the scent and the sample."*

During the Siege of Whitebread Square, an Aroma Keeper filled the streets with the fragrance of fresh sourdough while a Taste Tester offered free samples from a silver tray. Entire enemy battalions abandoned their packaged bread and joined the Order before the battle even began.

**Effect**: +15% damage to both towers. Slow keeps targets in range longer for more poison ticks.

### Grand Opening Rush

*"When every craft joins the feast, the streets move faster."*

The Order celebrates new bakeries with a chaotic festival involving monks, couriers, bakers, sages, and trucks all working at once. Nobody knows who is in charge, but bread appears everywhere.

**Effect**: +10% fire rate to all towers if 3+ tower types are on the map.
