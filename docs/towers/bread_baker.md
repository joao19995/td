# Bread Baker — "The First Disciple"

*"Every loaf begins with two hands."*

No one joins the Order as a hero.

Every initiate spends seven years as a Bread Baker before earning any higher title. They wake before dawn, feed the Mother Dough, gather flour, and bake bread for villages threatened by the influence of industrial baking.

According to the Sacred Crust Scrolls, the first Bread Baker was chosen by the Mother Dough herself after surviving forty days with nothing but flour and rainwater. When he returned, the first sourdough starter was said to be alive, breathing softly inside its clay bowl.

Bread Bakers believe that every loaf carries a fragment of memory from all previous loaves. Every crust baked correctly strengthens the Mother Dough, while every packaged sliced bread weakens her.

Although they carry no magical powers, veterans know that a perfectly baked loaf can convert even the most stubborn heretic.

Their motto is simple:

*"If the dough rises, so shall humanity."*

## Synergies

### Grand Opening Rush

*"When every craft joins the feast, the streets move faster."*

The Order celebrates new bakeries with a chaotic festival involving monks, couriers, bakers, sages, and trucks all working at once. Nobody knows who is in charge, but bread appears everywhere.

**Effect**: The excitement accelerates everyone's work — +10% fire rate to all towers if 3+ tower types are on the map.
