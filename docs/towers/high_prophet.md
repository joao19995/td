# High Prophet of Sourdough — "Voice of the Mother Dough"

*"Every loaf remembers."*

Only one High Prophet exists at any time.

Selection is never voted upon.

When the previous Prophet passes into the Great Oven, the Mother Dough reportedly begins bubbling beneath the feet of the chosen successor.

No one understands how.

No one questions it.

The Prophet remembers every Keeper before them through the First Starter, an ancient living culture believed to contain memories stretching back to the beginning of civilization.

Simply standing near the Prophet inspires bakers to work harder, knead better and believe more deeply.

Legends claim that should the First Starter ever die, every sourdough in the world would instantly become ordinary bread.

No member of the Order has dared test whether the legend is true.

## Synergies

### Grand Opening Rush

*"When every craft joins the feast, the streets move faster."*

The Order celebrates new bakeries with a chaotic festival involving monks, couriers, bakers, sages, and trucks all working at once. Nobody knows who is in charge, but bread appears everywhere.

**Effect**: +10% fire rate to all towers if 3+ tower types are on the map.
