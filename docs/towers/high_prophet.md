# High Prophet of Sourdough — "Voice of the Mother Dough"

*"One loaf feeds a village. One prophecy feeds generations."*

The High Prophet is the supreme leader of the Order of the Mother Dough. Every decree shapes the future of artisan baking, every sermon strengthens the faithful, and every appearance inspires bakers across the land. It is said that the Mother Dough herself whispers forgotten recipes into the Prophet's dreams, revealing truths lost since the First Oven.

Only one High Prophet exists at any time.

Selection is never voted upon.

When the previous Prophet passes into the Great Oven, the Mother Dough reportedly begins bubbling beneath the feet of the chosen successor.

No one understands how.

No one questions it.

The Prophet remembers every Keeper before them through the First Starter, an ancient living culture believed to contain memories stretching back to the beginning of civilization.

Simply standing near the Prophet inspires bakers to work harder, knead better and believe more deeply.

Legends claim that should the First Starter ever die, every sourdough in the world would instantly become ordinary bread.

No member of the Order has dared test whether the legend is true.

### Level I — Chosen Disciple

*"The Mother Dough has spoken."*

Before becoming Prophet, every chosen disciple spends years studying the oldest scriptures of fermentation, preserving traditions that predate kingdoms themselves.

Few are ever considered worthy of hearing the First Recipe.

### Level II — Sourdough Sermon

Cost: 176g — +10 Damage, +0.2 Fire Rate

*"Every sermon leaves another soul hungry for truth."*

The Prophet begins preaching throughout the kingdom, gathering followers with stories of slow fermentation and the dangers of industrial bread.

Every word strengthens conviction.

Every loaf becomes a lesson.

### Level III — Great Fermentation

Cost: 264g — +15 Damage, +0.3 Fire Rate

*"Faith rises, just as dough does."*

Having united monasteries across the land, the Prophet oversees the Great Fermentation—a sacred celebration held once every generation, where thousands of starters are renewed simultaneously.

The air itself seems filled with purpose.

### Level IV — Voice of the Mother Dough

Cost: 352g — +20 Damage, +0.3 Fire Rate

*"The faithful no longer follow the Prophet. They follow the voice behind him."*

At this stage, the Prophet's sermons become legendary. Entire villages abandon processed bread after hearing only a single sentence.

Even veteran heretics begin questioning everything they once believed.

### Level V — Living Prophet of the First Oven

Cost: 462g — +30 Damage, +0.4 Fire Rate

*"The First Oven still burns... and its flame walks among us."*

Only one Living Prophet exists in any generation.

Legends claim the Mother Dough no longer speaks through dreams, but directly through the Prophet himself. Wherever they walk, forgotten starters awaken, abandoned ovens reignite, and the faithful kneel in silent reverence.

Even the Industrial Bread Dragon is said to have paused upon hearing the Prophet's final sermon.

## Synergies

### Grand Opening Rush

*"When every craft joins the feast, the streets move faster."*

The Order celebrates new bakeries with a chaotic festival involving monks, couriers, bakers, sages, and trucks all working at once. Nobody knows who is in charge, but bread appears everywhere.

**Effect**: +10% fire rate to all towers if 3+ tower types are on the map.
