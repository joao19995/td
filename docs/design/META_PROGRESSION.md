# Meta-Upgrades

Permanent upgrades purchased with tokens between runs. **65 items total**, organized in 9 categories.

---

## Tower Upgrades — Balance Notes

Several tier-1 towers (Baker, Courier, Aroma Keeper) have cheap initial upgrades that the player can reach in 2–3 waves. Expensive towers (Prophet, Exorcist, Crusader) require multi-fight saving. The *per-tower unlock cost* acts as a gate so the player invests in a specific tower before unlocking its upgrade path.
